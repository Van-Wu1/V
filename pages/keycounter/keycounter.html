Dashboard仪表盘

热力图、键位排行、鼠标明细与趋势图集中在一个面板里。 Heatmap, key ranking, mouse detail, and trend views in one place.

Widget悬浮框

桌面常驻显示今日 Keys / Mouse，并支持快速右键操作。 A floating desktop widget for live Keys / Mouse tracking.

Health健康提醒

久坐、腱鞘炎、喝水三类提醒，尽量轻量，不强打断。 Sitting, tenosynovitis, and water reminders without heavy interruption.

Language双语
