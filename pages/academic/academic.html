Driven by a lifelong fascination with geography since high school, I specialize in bridging the gap between Urban Spatial Science and Visual Storytelling.

My academic journey began at Nanjing University of Information Science and Technology, where I earned double degrees in GIS and Human-Physical Geography. Currently, I am advancing my research at CASA within The Bartlett, UCL.

自高中起便深爱地理，热衷于以地理视角解构世界。我的核心研究方向在于将城市空间科学与数据可视化深度链接，以此洞察城市运作的隐形脉络。

本科双学位毕业于南京信息工程大学（GIS 与 人文地理与自然地理）。目前深造于 UCL 巴特莱特学院 CASA 实验室，致力于通过数据流动的视角探究城市形态。
